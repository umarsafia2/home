Hogwarts Houses

Introduction

The Hogwarts Houses are the 4 houses that kids at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, a wizarding school in the Harry Potter world, are divided into based on their characteristics. The names of these houses are based on the names of the four founders of Hogwarts. All four of the houses play an important role in Hogwarts’ history, and J.K. Rowling provides evidence of that in the seven Harry Potter books.

Characteristics of Each One

  1. Slytherin

    One of the four houses at Hogwarts, Slytherin was founded by Salazar Slytherin. Slytherins tend to be cunning, ambitious, determined, and strong leaders. These were all qualities possessed both by Draco Malfoy and Tom Riddle (Voldemort). Slytherin colours are green and silver, and the emblematic animal associated with them is a snake. Horace Slughorn and Severus Snape were the two notable heads of the house.

  2. Gryffindor

    One of the four houses at Hogwarts, Gryffindor was founded by Godric Gryffindor . Gryffindors tend to be brave, courageous, and determined, qualities possessed by Harry, Hermione, Ron, Neville, and all other Gryffindors. They have a fierce rivalry with the Slytherins. The emblematic animal associated with them is a lion and their house’s colours are scarlet and gold.

  3. Hufflepuff

    One of the four houses at Hogwarts, Hufflepuff was founded by Helga Hufflepuff . Students belonging to this house tend to be hard-working, friendly, loyal, honest, and impartial, qualities possessed by Cedric Diggory. They are not as competitive as the other houses. Their emblematic animal is a badger, and their house colours are yellow and black.

  4. Ravenclaw

    One of the four houses at Hogwarts, Ravenclaw was founded by Rowena Ravenclaw. Students belonging to Ravenclaw tend to be academically motivated and talented, qualities possessed by characters like Luna Lovegood. Their emblematic animal symbol is an eagle, and the house’s colours are blue and bronze.
